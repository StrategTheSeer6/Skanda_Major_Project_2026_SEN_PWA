About This App

SEN is a web app that focuses on helping students and teachers better understand the Year 11 Preliminary Software Engineering course through the use of Exam-Style Questions. This app is completely free and a project that Light Guard Studios has been working on for a long time, and has been able to draw the attention of students and teachers alike! All logged in users can use our free software by logging in or creating an account and connecting to a teacher account if they are a new student on the app. To get an idea on how our quiz software works, you can click the button below, which will redirect you to a demo test.
